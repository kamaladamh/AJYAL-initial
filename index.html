AJYAL Project Journey

Our process, from project wishes to a working facade.

AJYAL guides the full solar facade journey: mapping the brief, proving the business case, developing the right design, engineering the system, coordinating production, and supporting installation and maintenance.

01 Introduction & SolarCheck

An initial meeting maps project wishes, suitable colors, and design options. If needed, SolarCheck uses satellite and building data to estimate annual energy yield, facade orientation, optimal panel area, CO2 savings, investment, and return.

02 Bespoke Design & Colouring

AJYAL and Solarix support architects with a tailor-made design process, from collection-based concepts to unique patterns, colors, and samples that fit the project and Saudi architectural identity.

03 Engineering & SolarScan

Engineering support turns the concept into a workable facade approach with samples, digital files, shading study, design options, detailing, targeted cost indication, and payback calculation.

04 Production & Installation Planning

Solarix develops and manufactures the facade panels while AJYAL selects the right fixation system, coordinates the installation plan, and prepares building details for the specific project conditions.

05 Installation & Maintenance

AJYAL works with installation partners to deliver a complete, operating facade, then supports maintenance, repairs, replacement parts, and individual panel access where required.
