AJYAL Project Journey

Our process, from project wishes to a working facade.

AJYAL guides the full solar facade journey: mapping the brief, proving the business case, developing the right design, engineering the system, coordinating production, and supporting installation and maintenance.

  1. Introduction & SolarCheckAn initial meeting maps project wishes, suitable colors, and design options. If needed, SolarCheck uses satellite and building data to estimate annual energy yield, facade orientation, optimal panel area, CO2 savings, investment, and return.
  2. Bespoke Design & ColouringAJYAL and Solarix support architects with a tailor-made design process, from collection-based concepts to unique patterns, colors, and samples that fit the project and Saudi architectural identity.
  3. Engineering & SolarScanEngineering support turns the concept into a workable facade approach with samples, digital files, shading study, design options, detailing, targeted cost indication, and payback calculation.
  4. Production & Installation PlanningSolarix develops and manufactures the facade panels while AJYAL selects the right fixation system, coordinates the installation plan, and prepares building details for the specific project conditions.
  5. Installation & MaintenanceAJYAL works with installation partners to deliver a complete, operating facade, then supports maintenance, repairs, replacement parts, and individual panel access where required.
